Astronomical Events

An online calendar with solstices, equinoxes, and full & new moons. Subscribe in your calendar app of choice.

The calendar uses all-day events rather than precise times, so it needs a timezone. (The time is shown in each event's title.)

100-year feed recommended

~2,900 events · ~0.7 MB · loads reliably everywhere

1000-year feed

~28,800 events · ~6.7 MB · may be slow in some apps

Add to Google Calendar

  1. Open Google Calendar on the web.
  2. Next to Other calendars, click + → From URL.
  3. Paste a feed URL above and click Add calendar.
  4. To get emails, go into that calendar's settings and add an All-Day Event Notification.

Apple Calendar: File → New Calendar Subscription. Same URLs work.
